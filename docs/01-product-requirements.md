# Executive Summary

PrepOS AI is an AI-native Civil Services Preparation Operating System designed for:

* UPSC Aspirants
* APPSC Aspirants
* TSPSC Aspirants
* Coaching Institutes
* Faculties
* Mentors

The platform is not another learning portal or AI chatbot.

The platform acts as an intelligent preparation ecosystem that continuously tracks student learning, identifies weaknesses, plans study schedules, schedules revisions, analyzes previous year question trends, evaluates performance, and guides preparation through an AI Mentor.

The platform's long-term goal is to become the operating system for competitive exam preparation.

---

* What to study
* What to revise
* Why they are improving

without requiring manual planning.

# Core Product Engines

The product is built around eight intelligence engines.

---

## Engine 1

Syllabus Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* Store syllabus hierarchy
* Track syllabus evolution
* Create concept relationships

Example:

UPSC
GS1
Modern History
Revolt of 1857
Freedom Struggle

---

## Engine 2

Learning Graph Engine

Responsibilities:

* Track mastery
* Track confidence
* Track retention
* Track performance

Every student has a personal learning graph.

---

## Engine 3

PYQ Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* Analyze previous year questions
* Detect trends
* Calculate importance scores

Outputs:

* Topic importance
* Trend analysis
* Predicted relevance

---

## Engine 4

Revision Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* Schedule revisions
* Predict forgetting
* Optimize retention

Outputs:

* Daily revision plan
* Revision backlog
* Revision health score

---

## Engine 5

Mentor Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* Generate daily plans
* Generate weekly plans
* Generate monthly plans
* Recommend priorities

Acts as the CEO of the system.

---

## Engine 6

Assessment Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* MCQ evaluation
* Mains evaluation
* Mock analysis

Outputs:

* Weakness detection
* Improvement plans

---

## Engine 7

Knowledge Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* RAG
* Concept explanations
* Notes generation
* Current affairs support

This is a supporting engine.

Not the core product.

---

## Engine 8

Institution Intelligence Engine

Responsibilities:

* Batch analytics

* Institute analytics

# Preparation Twin

Every student receives a digital representation called:

Preparation Twin

The twin stores:

* Knowledge
* Retention
* Confidence
* Revision history
* Study habits
* Writing ability
* Assessment history

The twin evolves continuously.

All AI decisions operate on the Preparation Twin.
